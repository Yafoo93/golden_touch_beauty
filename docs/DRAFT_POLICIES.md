# Golden Touch Beauty Centre — Draft Development Policies

> [!WARNING]
> These policies are provisional content for development and testing. They are not legal advice and must be reviewed, completed, and approved by Golden Touch Beauty Centre and an appropriately qualified Ghanaian legal/privacy adviser before publication or production launch.

## Terms and Conditions

### 1. About the platform

The Golden Touch Beauty Centre platform allows customers to view services and products, request appointments, make eligible payments, purchase products, and manage related account activity. Use of the platform is subject to these terms and the policies referenced below.

### 2. Customer accounts

Customers must provide accurate and current information and keep account credentials confidential. Activity performed through an account may be treated as authorized unless Golden Touch is notified promptly of suspected unauthorized access.

### 3. Service information

Service descriptions, durations, and prices may vary according to the customer’s needs, treatment option, materials, group size, location, or package. Where a listing displays a starting price, range, or quotation requirement, the final amount will be confirmed before payment or service delivery.

### 4. Health and treatment information

Customers should provide accurate information about allergies, medical conditions, previous treatments, reactions, pregnancy, medications, or other factors relevant to a requested treatment. Golden Touch may decline, postpone, modify, or recommend professional medical advice where a treatment may be unsuitable. Website information does not replace medical advice.

### 5. Appointments

Submitting a preferred date and time does not guarantee availability. Golden Touch may approve the request, propose another time or branch, or reject it. A proposed change becomes confirmed only after the customer accepts it where acceptance is required.

### 6. Payments

Available payment methods are displayed during booking, checkout, or POS payment. Electronic payments are confirmed only after successful provider verification. Pay at Clinic bookings remain subject to management confirmation. Customers are responsible for any outstanding balance displayed on an appointment, order, or receipt.

### 7. Products and availability

Product acceptance is subject to price and stock verification. Golden Touch may cancel and refund an order when a product is unavailable, incorrectly priced, or cannot be fulfilled. Product appearance may differ slightly from photography due to lighting, packaging changes, or screen settings.

### 8. Acceptable use

Users must not attempt unauthorized access, interfere with the platform, submit false payment evidence, abuse booking processes, scrape protected information, or use the service unlawfully.

### 9. Limitation and updates

Golden Touch may update operational content and these terms. Material changes should be communicated appropriately. Any limitation-of-liability wording must be finalized through legal review and must not exclude rights that cannot lawfully be excluded.

## Appointment Cancellation and Refund Policy

### Customer cancellations

Customers should cancel or request rescheduling as early as possible using the account dashboard or an approved contact channel. The final notice period and any cancellation fee must be inserted before production.

### Rescheduling

Rescheduling is subject to availability. Management may propose another time or branch. A revised appointment is confirmed after required customer acceptance.

### Late arrival and no-show

Late arrival may shorten the service, require rescheduling, or result in cancellation where the remaining time is insufficient. The production policy must state the applicable grace period and any fee or forfeiture rule. A customer who does not attend may be marked as a no-show.

### Refunds

Approved refunds should return through the original payment method where possible. Provider and banking timelines may apply. Completed services are not normally refundable merely because results differ from subjective expectations, but complaints, adverse reactions, or service-quality concerns will be reviewed fairly.

### Consultation fee

The PRD specifies that the separate GHS 200 consultation fee is non-refundable. This rule must be displayed clearly before consultation payment.

### Golden Touch cancellations

If Golden Touch cannot provide a confirmed service, management may propose another date, time, branch, or appropriate refund. The customer will be notified using available contact details.

## Delivery and Returns Policy

### Payment and delivery quotation

Cash on delivery is not available. Products and any accepted delivery charge must be paid before dispatch. During Phase 1, Golden Touch may prepare delivery quotations manually based on the destination and order.

### Addresses and delivery

Customers must provide a complete and accurate delivery address and reachable contact number. Delivery estimates are not guarantees where delays arise from carriers, customs, weather, incomplete information, or circumstances outside reasonable control.

### Clinic pickup

Only branches with sufficient available stock may be selected for pickup. Customers should wait for confirmation that an order is ready before visiting the branch.

### Return window

Return requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery or pickup. Customers must provide the order reference, reason, and any requested photographs or supporting information.

### Return condition

Unless the item is incorrect, damaged, defective, or otherwise approved by management, it should be unused, unopened, and in its original condition and packaging. Opened skincare, makeup, wigs, and other hygiene-sensitive or personal-use beauty products are not normally returnable.

### Approval and refunds

Every return requires management review and approval. Approved refunds should follow the original payment process where possible. Original delivery charges and return-delivery responsibility must be finalized before production.

### Preorders

Preorders require full payment. Estimated availability dates may change. Customers will be notified of material date changes and when stock becomes ready for pickup or dispatch.

## Privacy Policy

### Information collected

Golden Touch may collect identity and contact information, account credentials, addresses, booking information, treatment-relevant information, consent choices, payments, orders, communications, device/security information, and activity required to operate and protect the platform.

### Purposes

Information may be used to provide services and orders, process payments, communicate updates, maintain treatment and transaction history, protect accounts, prevent fraud, meet legal obligations, improve operations, and send marketing only where permitted.

### Sensitive information

Treatment, allergy, and relevant medical information must be restricted to authorized users and access-logged. It must not be exposed to cashiers, receptionists, or other staff beyond what their duties require.

### Photographs and marketing consent

Before-and-after photograph consent is optional, timestamped, and withdrawable. Marketing consent is separate from treatment-record storage. Withdrawal prevents future promotional use after the request is processed but may not require deletion of records that must lawfully or operationally be retained.

### Payments and service providers

Payment information is processed through approved payment providers such as Korapay. Golden Touch must not store raw card numbers or CVV values. Necessary information may be shared with hosting, email, storage, analytics, delivery, security, and professional service providers under appropriate safeguards.

### Retention and rights

The production policy must define retention periods and provide contact details for access, correction, consent withdrawal, deletion, restriction, objection, or complaint requests where applicable under Ghanaian law.

### Security and contact

Golden Touch will use reasonable organizational and technical safeguards, but no internet system is completely risk-free. Final privacy contact details, data-controller information, complaint procedure, and regulator references must be supplied before production.

## Website Disclaimer

- Service and product content is general information and does not constitute medical advice.
- Individual treatment and product results vary.
- Customers should seek qualified medical attention for urgent, severe, persistent, or diagnosed health conditions.
- Product ingredient, allergy, usage, and warning information must be checked against the actual packaging.
- Placeholder prices, stock, ratings, testimonials, images, and statistics used during development must not be treated as production commitments.
